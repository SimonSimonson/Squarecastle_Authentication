Game principle:

Each player is assigned a random card. He can then place it anywhere on the playing field. The goal is to collect as many points as possible. You get points by placing your card on a card that is already on the field.

There are different types of cards:

meadow, castle and road pieces. For example, a card can also show a meadow and castle. You can for example only place castle pieces on other castle pieces.

How to collect points:

For castle pieces you get 2 points credited to your account. For roads you get 1 point and for meadows 0 points. The longer the chain of cards, the more points you get. So if you add another card to two already adjoining parts of a castle, you will get 6 points in total.
